Cheesecake Recipe

Tarte portion:

Ingredients

  • butter - 60g
  • egg yolk - 1
  • sugar - 40g
  • flour - 120g

Steps

  1. preheat oven to 350 F
  2. put in food processor
  3. in a cake pan, press the mix to create a crust
  4. bake at 350 F for 10 min

Filling portion:

Ingredients

  • heavy cream - 1 cup
  • 2 eggs
  • cream cheese - 1 pack
  • flour - 3 TBSP
  • sugar - 2/3 cup
  • lemon juice - 1TBSP

Steps

  1. put in food processor
  2. pour onto crust
  3. bake at 350 F for about 30 min. time will depend on oven strength.
